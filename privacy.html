Privacy Policy for Gokil Super Slider

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Gokil Super Slider ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application Gokil Super Slider (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Game Progress: Level completion status and best move counts are stored locally on your device.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and advertising purposes.
  • Usage Data: Game statistics such as levels played and time spent in-app.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage data

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Save your game progress locally
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Analyze app usage patterns

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Game progress (best moves per level) is stored locally on your device.
  • No Account Required: Gokil Super Slider does not require user registration or account creation.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number.

Children's Privacy

Gokil Super Slider is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game displays ads through AdMob, which complies with COPPA regulations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • Third-party advertising partners (AdMob) as described above
  • When required by law

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Clear local game data by uninstalling the app
  • Reset advertising identifier through your device settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads through device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in this location.
